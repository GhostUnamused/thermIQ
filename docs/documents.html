Knowledge Base Documents

ThermIQ measures a plant's knowledge gaps by comparing what the plant has documented against what the CEA standard says a plant should document. The two source types below serve different roles — keep them separate.

📐

Benchmark Sources Yardstick

CEA standards and regulatory guidelines that define what a well-run 500MW plant should document and maintain. These are the measuring stick — they don't change per plant.

🔒 Locked — cannot be deleted. Changes to the benchmark would change the assessment baseline for every plant.

🏭

Client Plant Sources Being Assessed

A specific plant's own operational documents (SOPs, manuals, tariff petitions, inspection records). These are what's being measured. Gaps = topics the benchmark requires that these documents don't cover.

✏️ Uploadable and deletable. Deleting triggers gap recomputation.
